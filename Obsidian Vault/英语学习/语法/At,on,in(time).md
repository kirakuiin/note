对于时间来说：
- **at**用于一天的某个时间点。
> [!example]
> at five o'clock.
> at sunset.
> at 11.45
- **on**用于某一天和日期。
> [!example]
> on Friday.
> on 16 May 2009.
- **in**用于强调事情在一个时间段内
> [!example]
> in a day.
> in October.
> in Winter.
> in the 18th century.

上面的规则中有一些特例：
- 圣诞节，周末用**at**。at Christmas, at the weekend.
- 使用in the morning但是on Friday morning.

> [!hint]
> 如果时间前有**last/next/this/every**，此时省略介词。
> > [!example]
> > I'll see you next Friday.

> [!hint] In a few minutes
> 这个短语代表的含义是从现在开始经过多久。
> > [!example]
> > Andy has gone away. He'll be back in a week. (a week from now)
